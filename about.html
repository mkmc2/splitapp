About Us

What we're doing.

At Split, we believe that raffles are outdated and boring. The entire process behind raffles hasn't been updated until now. We have developed a new innovative mobile application that is aimed at making raffles more exciting, beneficial, and seamless for everyone involved. Everything is done over your mobile device - no more missing the game winning goal while trying to buy a ticket.
In fact, were so passionate about revolutionizing raffles, we decided to change the name. Even though a 'raffle' and a 'Split' are technically the same thing, since we're updating so much already, we thought a name change was appropriate. Our app will change the raffle game forever.

How it works

1. Sponsors Start Splits

Any qulaified organization is allowed to sponsor a raffle - ranging from a professional sports team to your local Little League team, and everything in between.

2. Users Join Splits

Users are alerted when a raffle has started or can view all open raffles over the app. To join, they simply click "Join Split" to purchase their tickets.

3. Everyone Wins

Once the timer runs out, the application automatically selects a winner. The pot is then split between the winner and a charity. Even if you don't win the jackpot, you're supporting a good cause!

SPLITS ARE ALL AROUND YOU

Commitment to Charities.

Everything we do is aimed at helping our charities. For too long the process for charities to host raffles has been expensive and extremely labor intensive. Our new app changes all of that.
It is Split's mission to completely eliminate all costs and efforts put forth by charities to host raffles, by rewarding organizations who help with rundraising.
